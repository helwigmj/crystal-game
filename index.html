Crystal Collector

How to play:


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


The four crystals below are also given a random number- each time you click a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number,but if your total score gets higher than the random number, you lose.


The value of each crystal is hiddenf orm you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
